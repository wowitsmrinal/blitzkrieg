Blue's Turn

Rules

  • Blue goes first, then green.
  • Achieve higher total square value, when all places have been occupied.
  • Squares top, left, bottom and right of a square are adjacent to it.
  • In each turn, you can capture an unoccupied square, which is
    • Not adjacent to any of your pieces.
    • Adjacent to one of your pieces.
  • If the latter is used, you will also capture enemy squares adjacent to the newly captured square (non-recursively).

Configure

Alpha-Beta pruning is a faster version of the Minimax algorithm.
